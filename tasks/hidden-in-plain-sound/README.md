# Challenge: Hidden in Plain Sound

* **Points:** 20 Points
* **Difficulty:** Easy-Medium
* **Category:** Misc

## Description

Sometimes the most carefully hidden secrets are not buried deep or locked away, but hidden within the sounds we hear every day - silent not because they are hidden, but because they are disguised as something familiar.

What seems like just another familiar melody, can hide messages quietly in places you never expect, carried by sounds beyond perception.
